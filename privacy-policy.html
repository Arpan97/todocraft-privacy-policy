Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the TodoCraft – Tasks & Planner app (the "Application") created by Arpan Govila ("Developer") as a free offline productivity tool. The app is provided "AS IS" and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect any personal data.

TodoCraft works entirely offline. All tasks, notes, settings, and related data are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

The Application:

  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT require an internet connection
  • Does NOT use analytics, crash reporting, or third-party SDKs
  • Does NOT share data with anyone

Permissions

TodoCraft may request minimal permissions (if any) only for features inside the app, such as saving app data. All information remains securely stored within the user’s device.

Third-Party Access

The Application does not use any external services, third-party APIs, or cloud storage. No data is shared with external companies or servers.

Data Storage and Retention

All data created within TodoCraft—such as tasks, reminders, and preferences—is stored **locally on your device**.

If the app is uninstalled, all data will be deleted permanently unless you manually back it up using your device’s storage system.

Data Deletion

You may delete your data at any time by:

  • Using the in-app delete features, or
  • Uninstalling the application from your device.

Since all data is stored locally, deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Children’s Privacy

The Application does not collect any data from users, including children under 13. TodoCraft is safe for general use and does not request personal or sensitive information.

Security

The security of your data depends on your device. As no information leaves your device, we recommend enabling device-level security features (PIN, fingerprint, face unlock, etc.) for protection.
